The Best Video Games According to Matthew

My top 3 video games of all-time.

There are so many amazing video games out there that it is so hard to pick favourites.
Doom (1993) was the first ever video game I played and I love the entire series of releases.
The Wolfenstein series too! However, some of the best games I've ever played have been more recent.

Terraria

This is my favourite game. I have invested more time than I would like to mention into the progression and the various features.
This game is satisfying, has a lot of replayability, and is enjoyed even more with the wide veriaty of community-made mods.

Wolfenstein - Enemy Territory

An old but gold game. The simple shoot-em-up gameplay in a multiplayer matchup sometimes ends up with you finding friends and nemeses.
I ended up joining and socialising with various communities of people in the game, and even built my own.

Genshin Impact

A very beautiful, in-depth, open-world game with a long and immersive story and a lot of options when it comes to how you play it.
It's amazing figuring out how combinations of different characters, weapons and artifacts work best in different scenarios.
You'll find yourself emotionally invested in the charaters and stories of the world in this game, and all of a sudden there's an evening gone.
